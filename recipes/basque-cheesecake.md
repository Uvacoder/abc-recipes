# Basque cheesecake

Butter + parchement a cake pan.

- 680g cream cheese
- 225g mascarpone

Combine and whisk on medium speed.

- 320g sugar

Gradually add until smooth.

- 6 eggs
- 1 additional egg yolk

Add one at a time. Then add the additional yolk.

- 3/4 tsp fine sea salt
- 415 ml heavy cream
- 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 tsp orange blossom water
 
Add all to the mixture .

- 50g flour

Sift in flour.

Bake for 205Â° C for about an hour. It should look burnt.
